About Anderlecht Community NGO

Anderlecht Community NGO is a local non-profit organisation that supports community building and helps people who have fewer financial and social resources. The organisation works with volunteers, local partners and neighbourhood initiatives to organise events, provide support and strengthen social connections in Anderlecht.
